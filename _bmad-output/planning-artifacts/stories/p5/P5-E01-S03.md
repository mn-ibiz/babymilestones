# P5-E01-S03 — Group session booking

**Status:** Ready for development
**Phase:** P5 — Coaching, eTIMS, SMS Go-Live, Polish
**Epic:** P5-E01 — Mom Coaching & Birth Doula
**Estimate:** 1–3 days

## Job To Be Done

As a parent, I want to attend a group session (e.g., "Newborn Care, Saturday") with other parents.

## Acceptance Criteria

- AC1: Group sessions defined as slots with capacity > 1.
- AC2: Parents book individual seats; bookings list shows seats remaining.
- AC3: Same payment and reminder flows as 1:1.
