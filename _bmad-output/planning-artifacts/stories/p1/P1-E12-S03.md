# P1-E12-S03 — Deep-link from WhatsApp ads

**Status:** Ready for development
**Phase:** P1 — Foundation + Wallet + Parent Account
**Epic:** P1-E12 — Marketing & Landing (public route group)

## Job To Be Done

As a marketing manager, I want to link from a WhatsApp ad straight to the right booking flow.

## Acceptance Criteria

- AC1: URL pattern `/book/[unit]?utm_*` captures UTM and pre-selects the unit.
- AC2: UTM persisted to parent on signup for attribution.

## Technical Notes
