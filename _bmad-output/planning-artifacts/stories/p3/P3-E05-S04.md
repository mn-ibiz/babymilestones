**Status:** Ready for development
**Phase:** P3 — Commission, Salon, Loyalty Engine, Reporting

## Job To Be Done

As accountant, I want to see how long outstanding balances have been open.

## Acceptance Criteria

- AC1: Buckets: 0–7, 8–30, 31–60, 61–90, 90+ days.
- AC2: Per-parent rows under each bucket; clickable to parent profile.
- AC3: CSV export.
